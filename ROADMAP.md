# Guardian Override Roadmap - v1.04

This roadmap shows where Guardian Override is headed. It highlights upcoming milestones, priorities, and the sequence of work needed to move from prototype to trusted family tool.

---

## Near-term (0-3 months)

- **Simulator improvements**
  - Add more rule types (time, activity, context-based).
  - Build a simple dashboard to preview multiple rules at once.
- **Logging and journaling**
  - Expand logs to show redirect history in plain language.
  - Create export options for family review (e.g., weekly summaries).
- **Contributor experience**
  - Simplify onboarding with clearer guides and examples.
  - Add starter issues for new contributors.

---

## Mid-term (3-6 months)

- **Extension pilot**
  - Release a working Chrome extension connected to the simulator.
  - Test redirect flows in real browsing scenarios.
- **Mobile prototype**
  - Expand Android skeleton with basic redirect triggers.
  - Begin parity planning for iOS.
- **Community governance**
  - Launch contributor voting on small roadmap items.
  - Publish quarterly transparency reports.

---

## Long-term (6-12 months)

- **Family dashboard**
  - Build a shared view for families to see patterns and discuss habits.
  - Include journaling prompts for reflection.
- **Accessibility and inclusivity**
  - Audit language, flows, and visuals for accessibility.
  - Add localization support for multiple languages.
- **Partnerships**
  - Explore collaborations with educators, therapists, and family support groups.
  - Pilot Guardian Override in small community settings.

---

## Vision (12+ months)

- **Cross-platform parity**
  - Full support across web, Android, and iOS.
- **Community marketplace**
  - Allow families and contributors to share redirect templates and journaling prompts.
- **Sustainable growth**
  - Introduce fair monetization and reward structures aligned with dignity-first values.
- **Trust and adoption**
  - Position Guardian Override as a trusted tool families recommend to each other.

---

## How to use this roadmap

- **Contributors:** Use this roadmap to see where help is most needed.
- **Families:** Understand what features are coming and how they align with our mission.
- **Maintainers:** Update this file regularly to reflect progress and changes.

---

## Updates

This roadmap is a living document. Priorities may shift based on feedback, testing, and community needs. Updates will be made openly and shared with contributors.
