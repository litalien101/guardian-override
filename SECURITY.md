# Guardian Override Security Policy - v1.0

Guardian Override is built to protect families and contributors.  
This document explains how we handle security, privacy, and responsible reporting.

---

## Our pledge

- **Transparency:** We will be clear about how data is handled and what protections are in place.
- **Privacy:** We do not use hidden monitoring or surveillance. Boundaries are transparent and consent-based.
- **Safety:** We treat security issues seriously and respond promptly.
- **Respect:** Reports are handled confidentially and without retaliation.

---

## Reporting a vulnerability

If you discover a security issue:

1. **Do not open a public issue.**  
   Instead, report privately to the maintainers.

2. **Send details to:**  
   `security@guardianoverride.org` (or the designated private channel).

3. **Include information such as:**
   - Description of the issue
   - Steps to reproduce
   - Impact or risk
   - Any suggested fixes

We will acknowledge receipt within 72 hours and provide updates as we investigate.

---

## Scope

This policy applies to:

- Guardian Override repositories and code
- Extensions and mobile prototypes
- Documentation and community platforms connected to the project

---

## Handling reports

- **Triage:** We review and confirm the issue.
- **Fix:** We prioritize and resolve confirmed vulnerabilities.
- **Communicate:** We share updates with the reporter and, when appropriate, the community.
- **Credit:** With consent, we acknowledge contributors who report responsibly.

---

## Security practices

- **Code reviews:** All changes are reviewed before merging.
- **Dependencies:** We monitor and update third-party packages regularly.
- **Access control:** Contributor permissions are limited to what is necessary.
- **Transparency:** Security-related changes are documented in release notes.

---

## Immediate safety

If you believe a vulnerability poses an immediate risk to families or contributors, please mark your report as **urgent**. We will prioritize it accordingly.

---

## Updates

This policy is a living document. As Guardian Override grows, our security practices will evolve. Updates will be made openly and shared with contributors.
