# Guardian Override Governance v1.3_draft

This document explains how decisions are made within Guardian Override. It is designed to be clear, fair, and transparent so that contributors understand how responsibilities are shared and how the community stays aligned.

---

## Guiding principles

- **Transparency:** Decisions are documented and shared openly.
- **Fairness:** Input is valued based on contribution and impact, not status.
- **Accountability:** Commitments are honored, and changes are explained.
- **Dignity:** Every contributor is treated with respect and is never disposable.
- **Sustainability:** Governance supports long-term growth and community trust.

---

## Roles

- **Founders:** Provide vision, set direction, and hold initial equity.
- **Maintainers:** Oversee repositories, review contributions, and ensure standards are followed.
- **Contributors:** Participate through code, design, documentation, governance, or outreach.
- **Community members:** Engage in discussions, provide feedback, and help shape priorities.

---

## Decision-making

- **Consensus first:** We aim for agreement through discussion and collaboration.
- **Maintainer responsibility:** Maintainers make final calls on technical and operational decisions when consensus is not possible.
- **Founder oversight:** Founders may step in for major strategic or mission-related decisions.
- **Documentation:** All decisions are recorded in issues, pull requests, or meeting notes.

---

## Areas of governance

- **Technical:** Code, architecture, testing, and infrastructure.
- **Design:** User experience, onboarding, accessibility, and documentation.
- **Community:** Standards, onboarding guides, conflict resolution, and conduct.
- **Outreach:** Partnerships, communication, and community building.

---

## Equity and profit sharing

- **Equity holders:** Have voting rights in major decisions and share in long-term value.
- **Profit-sharing participants:** Receive financial rewards but do not hold ownership or voting rights.
- **All contributors:** Agree to follow the [Community Code of Conduct](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md) and uphold Guardian Overrideâ€™s values.

---

## Conflict resolution

- **Step 1:** Raise the concern in the appropriate channel (issue, discussion, or meeting).
- **Step 2:** Seek resolution through respectful dialogue.
- **Step 3:** If unresolved, maintainers mediate and propose a fair solution.
- **Step 4:** Founders may provide final resolution if needed.

---

## Updates

This document is a living agreement. As Guardian Override grows, governance structures may evolve. Updates will be made openly and with input from contributors.
